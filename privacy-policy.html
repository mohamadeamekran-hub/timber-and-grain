Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Bois Créations SARL (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website boiscreations.ma (the “Site”) and make purchases from us.

Our registered business address is: Bois Créations SARL, Avenue Mohammed V, Tangier 90000, Morocco.
RC: 12345 (Tangier) — ICE: 001234567000089

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you place an order, create an account, or contact us, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Billing and shipping address
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe — we never store your full credit card number on our servers)
  • Order history and preferences
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide via contact forms, emails, WhatsApp, or live chat

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you browse our Site, we automatically collect certain data, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search engine
  • Device type and screen resolution
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 7 below)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Order fulfillment: Processing your orders, arranging shipping via our carriers (Amana Express, Aramex, DHL), sending order confirmations and tracking information
  • Customer service: Responding to inquiries, resolving complaints, and providing support
  • Payment processing: Securely processing transactions in MAD (Moroccan Dirham) through our payment partner Stripe
  • Communication: Sending transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping updates) and, where you have opted in, marketing communications
  • Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve our Site, products, and services
  • Legal compliance: Meeting our legal and regulatory obligations under Moroccan law
  • Fraud prevention: Detecting and preventing fraudulent transactions

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Payment processor: Stripe, for secure payment processing
  • Shipping carriers: To deliver your orders (Amana Express, Aramex, DHL)
  • Email service providers: To send transactional and marketing emails
  • Analytics providers: To understand how our Site is used (e.g., Google Analytics, with IP anonymization enabled)
  • Legal authorities: When required by Moroccan law, regulation, or legal proceedings

4. Payment Security

Your payment security is our top priority. All payment transactions are:

  • Encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS (HTTPS) technology
  • Processed by PCI DSS Level 1 compliant payment provider (Stripe)
  • Denominated in MAD (Moroccan Dirham)
  • Never stored on our servers — we only retain a transaction reference and the last four digits of your card for order management

We comply with Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards (PCI DSS) to ensure your financial data is handled safely.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes described in this policy
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax and accounting records are kept for the legally required period under Moroccan law)
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us (see Section 9).

6. Your Rights

Under Moroccan Law 09-08 on the Protection of Individuals with Regard to the Processing of Personal Data, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Objection: Object to our processing of your data, including direct marketing
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw any previously given consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@boiscreations.ma or write to us at our business address. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Essential cookies: Enable core functionality such as shopping cart, checkout, and account login
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our Site (Google Analytics with IP anonymization)
  • Marketing cookies: Used only with your explicit consent to show relevant promotions

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings or via our cookie consent banner displayed on your first visit. Disabling essential cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our Site.

8. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., social media platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@boiscreations.ma
  • General inquiries: contact@boiscreations.ma
  • Phone: +212 539 000 000

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of our Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. By clicking “Accept,” you consent to our use of cookies. Read our Cookie Policy for more information.
