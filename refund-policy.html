Refund, Return, Shipping & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

At Bois Créations, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This page outlines all our fulfillment policies in one place so you can shop with confidence. All prices, costs, and refunds are displayed and processed in MAD (Moroccan Dirham).

1. Shipping & Delivery Policy

1.1 Processing Time

As our products are handcrafted, please allow the following processing times before shipment:

  • Standard items (earrings, pens, atomizers, coasters, corkscrews, phone stands): 2–5 business days
  • Wall clocks: 5–10 business days
  • Custom orders: 10–20 business days (confirmed at time of order)

You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order is dispatched.

1.2 Shipping Rates and Delivery Times

Destination Method Cost (MAD) Estimated Delivery
Tangier (Local) Standard 20 MAD 1–2 business days
Tangier (Local) Express 50 MAD Same day / Next day
Morocco (National) Standard 35 MAD 2–5 business days
Morocco (National) Express 80 MAD 1–2 business days
Europe Standard 120 MAD 7–14 business days
Rest of World Standard 200 MAD 14–25 business days

Free shipping on all orders over 1,000.00 MAD (standard shipping within Morocco).

1.3 Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive an email containing your tracking number and a link to track your shipment in real time. If you do not receive tracking information within the expected processing window, please contact us at contact@boiscreations.ma.

1.4 International Orders

  • Customs duties, import taxes, and fees for orders shipped outside Morocco are the sole responsibility of the buyer
  • These fees are determined by your country's customs office and are not included in our prices
  • We are not responsible for delays caused by customs processing

1.5 Lost or Damaged Shipments

If your order arrives damaged or does not arrive within the estimated delivery window:

  1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery (for damage) or after the estimated delivery window has passed (for lost items)
  2. Provide your order number and photos of any damage (including packaging)
  3. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no extra cost

2. Return Policy

2.1 Return Eligibility

You may return most items within 30 days of delivery, provided:

  • The item is unused, unworn, and in its original condition
  • The item is in its original packaging
  • You have your order confirmation or receipt

2.2 Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Custom or personalized orders (e.g., engraved items)
  • Earrings and jewelry (for hygiene reasons, unless defective)
  • Items marked as “Final Sale”
  • Items that show signs of use, damage caused by the buyer, or missing original packaging

2.3 Return Shipping

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the buyer, unless the item is defective or we made an error. In those cases, Bois Créations will cover the return shipping cost or provide a prepaid return label.

2.4 How to Return

  1. Contact us: Email returns@boiscreations.ma with your order number and reason for return
  2. Receive a Return Authorization: We will provide a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and return shipping instructions within 2 business days
  3. Ship the item: Pack the item securely in its original packaging and ship it to the address provided. Include the RMA number on the outside of the package
  4. Receive your refund: Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund (see Section 3 below)

3. Refund Policy

3.1 Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item and confirm it meets our return conditions:

  • Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days
  • The refund will be issued to your original payment method via Stripe
  • You will receive an email confirmation when your refund is processed
  • Please allow an additional 3–10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement, depending on your bank or payment provider

3.2 Refund Amounts

Scenario Refund Amount
Standard return (item in original condition) Full product price in MAD (excluding original shipping)
Defective or damaged item Full product price + original shipping cost in MAD
Incorrect item received Full product price + original shipping cost + return shipping reimbursed in MAD
Order cancelled before shipment Full refund including shipping in MAD

3.3 Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. To exchange an item, please return it for a refund and place a new order for the desired item.

4. Cancellation Policy

4.1 Before Shipment

You may cancel your order at any time before it has been shipped:

  • Contact us immediately at contact@boiscreations.ma, call +212 539 000 000, or WhatsApp +212 661 000 000
  • If the order has not yet been dispatched, we will cancel it and issue a full refund within 3–5 business days
  • If the item has already been dispatched, cancellation is not possible — please follow the return process in Section 2

4.2 Custom Orders

Custom or personalized orders can only be cancelled within 24 hours of placing the order, as production typically begins immediately. After 24 hours, custom orders are non-cancellable and non-refundable.

4.3 Seller Cancellation

We reserve the right to cancel orders in the event of:

  • Stock unavailability
  • Payment processing issues
  • Suspected fraudulent activity
  • Pricing errors

In all cases, you will be notified immediately and receive a full refund in MAD.
