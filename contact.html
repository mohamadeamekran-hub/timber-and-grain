Quick Answers

Common questions our customers ask

How long does shipping take?

Standard items ship within 2–5 business days. Delivery takes 3–7 days within Morocco, 7–14 days for international orders. All prices and shipping fees are displayed in MAD. See our shipping policy for full details.

Can I return my order?

Yes, most items can be returned within 30 days if unused and in original packaging. Refunds are processed in MAD to your original payment method. Contact us at returns@boiscreations.ma or see our return policy for conditions.

Is my payment secure?

Absolutely. All transactions in MAD are encrypted with SSL/HTTPS and processed through Stripe and PayPal (PCI DSS Level 1 compliant). We never store your full card details and comply with Moroccan data protection regulations.
